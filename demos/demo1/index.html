DEMO 1: CHOOSE CONTROL

The object below is continually changing in three of its characteristics:

  • Position (left/right),
  • Shape (wide/thin), and
  • Axis Orientation (up/down).





The slider control affects all three characteristics simultaneously when you drag the it left or right (try it!). You can use the slider to counteract the changes taking place to any one of the three characteristics. Choose one of the three characteristics and practice using the mouse to keep that characteristic from varying. When you are ready to record a run, click the Start button.

Data recording begins after a brief delay, intended to give you time to begin controlling. When recording begins, the color of the sphere changes from red to green.
